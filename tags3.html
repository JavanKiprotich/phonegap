UNCLOSED TAGS

The unclosed tags are "hr" and "br"

"hr" is a tag for generating a single line

"br" is used to break in between successive elements.

Attributes

Attributes are attached to elemnts to give more meaning and dynamicize the webpage.
They include; "alt" for alternative output when missing image and "url" to specify direction for img selection.Others are width , height, border,margin.
